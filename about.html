Delivery service

Weeegoo will provide a delivery service to our partner within an hour from the preparation time done.

Easy ordering process:

1- Customers enter a delivery address via the app or website and select a store nearby.
2- Store will accept the order and start preparing the products for a pickup.
3- weeegoo rider arrives at the right time to collect and delivers the order to the customer doorstep.
